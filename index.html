Our Products

Peary Lock

A simple, easy-to-use device that can be clipped onto any zipper, physically blocking the zipper slider from moving anywhere. This physical barrier can only be opened by inputting the correct combination, effectively crippling a thieving mind looking for a quick swipe. With the Peary Lock, you can keep your belongings safe and secure at all times. So you can travel with a peace of mind and not worry about the little things. Travel smart, travel safe, travel without worries.

Secures the contents of your bag

Peary Lock prevents opportunistic theft of your personal belongings. Slider zips on your bag cannot be unzipped when secured with Peary Lock!

Usage flexibility

Peary Lock can be used on any bags you own, regardless of the number of slider zips. Furthermore, it does not damage your bag!

Customizable and fashionable

With numerous designs available, you can choose your favourite Peary Lock designs and colours to enhance your travelling experience!

About us

How we started

At Caribou, our vision is for every traveler to have the best travel experience imaginable. We want to make traveling safe, fuss-free, and as cost-effective as possible.

We are travelers ourselves, and while traveling we met with the issue of securing the contents of a bag with a single slider zip. Out of this personal inconvenience, the Peary Lock concept was conceived and Caribou was born. Rooted in that beginning, we at Caribou strive to enhance every travel experience through innovative and useful consumer products.


Vision

For every traveller to have the best travel experience imaginable.


Mission

Making travel safe, fuss-free and cost-effective and facilitate building connections.
